Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about custom curtains and blinds in Dubai — pricing, installation, timeline, motorization, and more.

Curtains

Curtains

Pricing, installation, blackout options, and what to expect when you order custom curtains.

Curtains page

How much do custom curtains cost in Dubai?

Custom curtain pricing at Jan Furnishings depends on fabric, style, and window size. Single-window treatments start from AED 500. Full apartment packages start from AED 3,500, villa packages from AED 8,500. We provide an exact quote during your free home visit — no obligation.

How long does curtain installation take?

From your initial home visit to installation, most orders are completed within 5–7 working days. Same-week installation is available for standard orders. Large villa or multi-room projects may take 7–10 days.

Do you offer blackout curtains for bedrooms?

Yes. We offer full blackout curtains that block 99% of light, ideal for bedrooms, nurseries, and media rooms. Available in 50+ colours and fabrics, with motorized options for hard-to-reach windows.

Can I get curtains and blinds together?

Absolutely. Many clients choose a sheer-and-blackout combination, or curtains on some windows and blinds on others. Our consultant advises room-by-room during your free home visit. Combo packages save compared to ordering separately.

Do you remove old curtains?

Yes. We remove and dispose of your old curtains and fittings at no extra charge when you order new window treatments from Jan Furnishings. This includes taking down old tracks and brackets.

What areas do you serve?

Jan Furnishings serves all of Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah. Our consultants visit your home anywhere in these emirates for a free, no-obligation measurement and quote.

Blinds

Blinds

Types, pricing, climate suitability, and maintenance for custom blinds in Dubai.

Blinds page

What types of blinds do you offer in Dubai?

Jan Furnishings offers roller blinds, blackout blinds, wooden blinds, motorized blinds, and Venetian blinds. All are custom-cut to your exact window dimensions and professionally installed.

Are your blinds suitable for Dubai's climate?

Yes. All our blind fabrics are UV-blocking and heat-resistant, specifically selected for the UAE climate. They help reduce indoor temperatures and protect furniture from sun damage.

How much do roller blinds cost in Dubai?

Roller blinds at Jan Furnishings start from AED 350 per window, depending on size and fabric. We provide exact pricing during your free home measurement visit. View full packages for multi-room pricing.

Can blinds be motorized?

Yes. Most blind types can be motorized, allowing you to control them via remote, smartphone app, or voice commands through Alexa or Google Home. Motorization adds approximately AED 300–500 per window. See our motorized blinds page for full details.

How do I clean my blinds?

Our blinds are designed for easy maintenance. Most can be wiped with a damp cloth. We provide specific care instructions for each fabric type at installation.

Motorized

Motorized & Smart Home

Voice control, app control, costs, and smart home compatibility for motorized window treatments.

Motorized page

How much does motorization cost for curtains or blinds in Dubai?

Motorization adds approximately AED 300–500 per window on top of the curtain or blind cost. The exact price depends on the motor type, track length, and integration required. We provide a full quote during your free home visit.

Can I control motorized curtains with my voice?

Yes. Our motorized systems are compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit, allowing you to control your curtains and blinds by voice command.

Can I add motorization to my existing curtains?

In many cases, yes. If your existing curtain track is compatible, we can retrofit a motor. Our consultant will assess this during the free home visit. New motorized tracks are also available.

Which smart home systems do your motorized blinds integrate with?

Our motorized window treatments are compatible with Somfy, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit. We can also integrate with most KNX and home automation systems used in Dubai villas.

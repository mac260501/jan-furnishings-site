Control Methods

Four Ways to Control Your Windows

From simple remote control to full smart home automation — choose the level of convenience that suits your home.

Remote Control

Simple handheld remote. Open, close, or set your curtains and blinds to any position with one touch. No Wi-Fi or smartphone required — reliable and straightforward for every member of the household.

Smartphone App

Control from anywhere in the world. Schedule your curtains to open at sunrise and close at sunset. Monitor and adjust remotely while you're away from home — full convenience at your fingertips.

Voice Control

Compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit. Say "Alexa, open the curtains" or "Hey Google, close the blinds." Hands-free convenience for every room in your home.

Scheduled Automation

Set your curtains and blinds to operate on a daily schedule. Wake up to natural light streaming in, or keep rooms cool by automatically closing before peak heat hours in the Dubai afternoon.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most about motorized window treatments. Visit our full FAQ page for more.

How much does motorization cost for curtains or blinds in Dubai?

Motorization adds approximately AED 300–500 per window on top of the curtain or blind cost. The exact price depends on the motor type, track length, and integration required. We provide a full quote during your free home visit.

Can I control motorized curtains with my voice?

Yes. Our motorized systems are compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit, allowing you to control your curtains and blinds by voice command.

Can I add motorization to my existing curtains?

In many cases, yes. If your existing curtain track is compatible, we can retrofit a motor. Our consultant will assess this during the free home visit. New motorized tracks are also available.

Which smart home systems do your motorized blinds integrate with?

Our motorized window treatments are compatible with Somfy, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit. We can also integrate with most KNX and home automation systems used in Dubai villas.

Do motorized curtains require electricity?

Yes, motorized systems require a power source. Most use a discreet wired connection installed during fitting. Battery-powered and rechargeable options are also available for windows without nearby power points.

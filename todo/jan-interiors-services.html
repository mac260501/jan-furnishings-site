From a single feature wall to a complete villa renovation — every service is delivered with the same level of care, craftsmanship and attention to detail.

01
Signature Service

Bespoke
Wall Panels

Fluted · Slatted · 3D Relief · Acoustic

Wall panels are the fastest way to take a room from ordinary to extraordinary. Our panels are custom-designed and fabricated to your exact dimensions — not off-the-shelf. Whether you want the warmth of natural wood veneer slats, the architectural drama of 3D geometric relief, or the clean lines of painted fluted MDF, we design and install it to be seamless.

  • Fluted & ribbed MDF panels — primed, painted or wood-finish to any RAL colour
  • Natural wood veneer slat panels — oak, walnut, ash and smoked finishes
  • 3D geometric & relief panels — statement feature walls with real depth
  • Acoustic fabric panels — beauty meets sound control for home offices and media rooms
  • LED channel integration — built-in lighting for atmosphere and drama
  • Full installation — including skirting transitions, LED channels and trim detail
  • 2-year installation warranty on all panel work

03
Artisan Finishing

Premium
Painting

Venetian Plaster · Limewash · Ombré · Colour Drenching

Painting is far more than applying emulsion. Done well, it's a transformation — the right finish on the right surface can make a room feel larger, warmer, more intimate or more dramatic. Our skilled painters bring both technical precision and an artistic eye, delivering finishes from flawless flat emulsion to textured Venetian plaster and limewash with real depth.

  • Venetian plaster — multi-layer burnished finish for walls with texture and sheen
  • Limewash — aged, organic appearance with real depth and variation
  • Colour drenching — walls, ceiling and joinery in a continuous enveloping tone
  • Ombré & gradient walls — seamless tone transitions across a feature wall
  • Geometric feature painting — precise architectural shapes and panelling effects
  • Flawless emulsion — perfect prep, zero brush marks, clean lines every time
  • All surfaces protected and all furniture covered during work

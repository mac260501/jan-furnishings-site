About Jan Furnishings

Built to remove the stress from curtains and blinds.

We created Jan Furnishings after seeing how complicated window furnishing felt for most homeowners. Too many unclear options, confusing pricing, and rushed decisions. Our model is simple: expert guidance, transparent quotes, and professional installation from one accountable team.

Our story

We started in the UAE furnishing space in 2021 through our sister brand, Al Hadeeqa. As we worked with more clients, one issue came up repeatedly: people wanted a trusted team for curtains and blinds, not another fragmented vendor experience.

Jan Furnishings was launched to solve that gap. Today we help clients across Dubai and nearby cities choose products that look right, perform well in local weather, and stay within budget.

Every project follows the same rule: no pressure, no guesswork, and no hidden fees.

What stays non-negotiable

  • Clear communication from first inquiry to after-sales support
  • Accurate in-home measurement before final quotation
  • Materials selected for heat, sun exposure, and daily use
  • Installation completed by trained in-house professionals
  • 12-month warranty on fabric, rails, and manufacturing defects

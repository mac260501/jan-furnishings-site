FAQ

Common questions about pricing & the process.

Ask on WhatsApp
Is the estimate price guaranteed?
The calculator gives you a close ballpark based on standard dimensions and our base pricing. Your final quote is confirmed during the free home visit, where we take exact measurements. We don't charge more than the quoted amount after that.
Does installation cost extra?
No. Installation is always included in the price. There are no delivery fees, no fitting charges, no extras. What you're quoted covers the full job from measurement to final install.
How does the free home visit work?
You book a slot online (takes under a minute). One of our experts shows up at your home with fabric samples, swatches, and a measuring tape. We help you choose the right product for your space, take all the measurements, and give you a precise quote on the spot. No commitment required.
What if my windows are non-standard sizes?
Everything we make is custom. Whether your windows are unusually narrow, extra tall, arched, or bay windows, we handle it. The calculator above adjusts the estimate based on what you enter, and our team confirms during the visit.
How long does delivery and installation take?
Once your order is confirmed and deposit paid, we typically install within 2–4 days. Our average is 48 hours. We'll confirm the installation date with you before we begin.
Do you offer motorized curtains?
Yes, motorized curtains and blinds start from AED 1,000. They're compatible with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit. The calculator above covers our most popular products — for motorized and premium options, book a visit and we'll quote on the spot.
What's covered under the warranty?
All products come with a 12-month warranty covering manufacturing defects and installation issues. If something isn't right after we install, we come back and fix it — no charge.

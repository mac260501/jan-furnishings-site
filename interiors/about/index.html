Chapter 01

It started with
Jan Furnishings.

Al Hadeeqa Contracting was founded in 2021 with a clear purpose: make it easier for people in Dubai to get their homes done properly. From that foundation, Jan Furnishings grew — bringing the same values of transparency, reliability and genuine craftsmanship specifically to curtains and blinds.

Over the following years, Jan Furnishings earned its reputation by doing exactly what it promised. Same-week turnarounds. Honest pricing. Installers who showed up when they said they would. The kind of service Dubai homeowners had come to expect from every other professional — but rarely found when it came to window treatments.

"Every visit told us the same thing: the walls mattered just as much as the windows."

But as the team spent more time inside clients' homes, something became clear. The curtains could be perfect. The blinds could be impeccable. And still, the room didn't feel finished — because the walls hadn't been touched in years, or the paint was the wrong tone, or the space lacked the texture and depth that separates a house from a home.

Chapter 02

A recurring
problem.

Dubai has no shortage of interior design firms. It also has no shortage of contractors who'll turn up, paint a wall, and leave before the client has noticed what's wrong. The problem isn't finding someone to do the work. It's finding someone who takes the work seriously enough to do it right.

Time and again, the team saw the same pattern. A client would commission a beautiful set of curtains — then struggle for months to find a panel installer who matched the quality. Or they'd have a vision for a feature wall and end up with something close but not right. The vision existed. The execution fell short. The gap between the two was where the frustration lived.

"We kept being asked: 'Do you know anyone good for the walls?' We realised the answer should be us."

Interiors work — real interiors work — requires a specific kind of attention. You can't rush a Venetian plaster finish. A wallpaper seam either disappears or it doesn't. A fluted panel installation is precise to the millimetre or it shows. This isn't a market where good enough is acceptable. It's a market where the best practitioners are quietly in demand and hard to find.

Chapter 03

The specialist
decision.

The decision to launch Jan Interiors as a distinct brand wasn't about expansion for its own sake. It was about being honest with clients and with ourselves. Curtains and interiors require different expertise, different materials, different teams, different timelines. Doing both under one roof is only worth doing if both are done to the same level of excellence.

So we built Jan Interiors properly. A dedicated design team. Craftspeople trained specifically in wall panels, decorative plaster, professional wallpaper installation and premium painting. A process that mirrors what made Jan Furnishings work: in-home consultation first, physical samples brought to you, nothing approved without your sign-off, nothing started without a detailed written quote.

"If we were going to do interiors, we were going to do interiors. Not as a side offering — as a specialism."

Jan Interiors isn't a rebrand. It isn't Jan Furnishings with a wallpaper catalogue added on. It is a specialist division — staffed differently, scoped differently, and held to the same uncompromising standard that built the group's reputation across more than 500 Dubai homes.

Chapter 04

What we stand
for today.

Jan Interiors handles wall panels, luxury wallpaper, premium painting and full villa renovation — all under one contract, with one team, and one project coordinator who answers when you call. We work across all Dubai areas, and our consultants come to you, not the other way around.

We believe the best interior work is invisible in the sense that it doesn't announce itself. It simply makes a room feel like it could never have been any other way. The fluted panels look like they grew from the architecture. The limewash finish has a depth that paint alone never achieves. The room is quiet, considered and complete.

"We don't just want the room to look finished. We want it to feel inevitable."

That's what we're building toward with every project. Not just a satisfied client — though that matters deeply — but a space that exceeds what the client imagined was possible when they first reached out.

Three Things We
Never Compromise On

01
Craft
Every finish we apply is one our team has been trained to execute properly. We don't cut corners on surface preparation. We don't rush wallpaper seams. We don't let a panel installation leave until the lines are straight. The craft either meets our standard or we do it again.

How We Work

The Approach That
Defines Every Project

We Come to You
Every project starts with a free in-home consultation. We don't ask you to come to a showroom. We come to your space — because your space is where the decisions need to be made. Lighting, dimensions, existing finishes: these only make sense in context.
You Approve Everything
No work begins without your written approval of the quote, the materials and the design direction. We prepare mood boards, bring physical samples and, for larger projects, produce 3D renders. What gets installed is exactly what you agreed to — no improvised decisions on site.
One Team, One Contract
We don't subcontract the parts that matter. The same team who consults with you coordinates the installation. For larger renovation projects, a dedicated project manager is your single point of contact from first visit through to handover. No broken telephone. No accountability gaps.
Warranted Work
Every project carries a warranty — 2 years on Canvas-level work, 3 years on Transformation, and 5 years on Masterwork renovation projects. If something isn't right, we return and make it right. No charge. No argument. This isn't a marketing promise; it's written into every quote we issue.

Three Brands.
One Standard.

Jan Interiors is a specialist division of Al Hadeeqa Contracting Co LLC — the same company behind Jan Furnishings, one of Dubai's most trusted names in custom curtains and blinds.

The Jan Group exists because a home is more than any single element. Windows, walls, structure — they work together. When the same trusted team handles more of a home, the result is more coherent, more considered, and far less stressful to coordinate.

Each brand in the group is a genuine specialism — not a catch-all contractor trying to do everything. Jan Furnishings focuses entirely on window treatments. Jan Interiors focuses entirely on wall finishes and interior renovation. Jan Construction handles construction, pergolas, carports and structural contracting. Specialists working alongside specialists.

About Our Curtains

Jan Furnishings provides custom-made curtains for homes across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah. We offer sheer, blackout, motorized, and combination curtains in 100+ premium fabrics. Every curtain is measured, cut, and installed by our team — with free home consultations and same-week installation available. If you are also considering custom blinds for your Dubai home, our consultants can advise on the best combination for each room.

What We Offer

Curtain Types

From light-filtering sheers to full motorized blackout — every style, made to measure.

Sheer Curtains

Light-filtering elegance. Let natural light in while maintaining privacy. Available in linen, silk-look, voile, and embroidered fabrics. Perfect for living rooms and dining areas. Pairs beautifully with blackout curtains for a layered look.

View sheer options

Blackout Curtains

Block 99% of light for complete darkness. Ideal for bedrooms, nurseries, and media rooms. Also provide thermal insulation, helping to reduce indoor temperatures in Dubai's climate. Available in 50+ colours.

View blackout options

Sheer & Blackout Combination

The most popular choice in Dubai. Double tracks allow you to switch between privacy-with-light during the day and full blackout at night. Custom-fitted for every window.

View combination options

Motorized Curtains

Control your curtains with a remote, smartphone app, or voice command. Compatible with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit. Perfect for high windows, large living rooms, and smart homes.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from Dubai homeowners. Visit our full FAQ page for more.

How much do custom curtains cost in Dubai?

Custom curtain pricing at Jan Furnishings depends on fabric, style, and window size. Single-window treatments start from AED 500. Full apartment packages start from AED 3,500, villa packages from AED 8,500. We provide an exact quote during your free home visit — no obligation.

How long does curtain installation take?

From your initial home visit to installation, most orders are completed within 5–7 working days. Same-week installation is available for standard orders. Large villa or multi-room projects may take 7–10 days.

Do you offer blackout curtains for bedrooms?

Yes. We offer full blackout curtains that block 99% of light, ideal for bedrooms, nurseries, and media rooms. Available in 50+ colours and fabrics, with motorized options for hard-to-reach windows.

Can I get curtains and blinds together?

Absolutely. Many clients choose a sheer-and-blackout combination, or curtains on some windows and blinds on others. Our consultant advises room-by-room during your free home visit. Combo packages save compared to ordering separately.

Do you remove old curtains?

Yes. We remove and dispose of your old curtains and fittings at no extra charge when you order new window treatments from Jan Furnishings. This includes taking down old tracks and brackets.

What areas do you serve?

Jan Furnishings serves all of Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah. Our consultants visit your home anywhere in these emirates for a free, no-obligation measurement and quote.
